Scripture Study Reflections

Thoughts related to temple and family history work, inspired by study.

"And he shall turn the heart of the fathers to the children, and the heart of the children to their fathers."
Malachi 4:6
This scripture reminds me that temple and family history work is not a separate duty, but a healing work. When we do ordinances for our ancestors, we participate in the Savior’s effort to bind families eternally. The Spirit of Elijah softens our hearts in ways we can feel—but also in quiet ways we may not notice right away.
"For my soul delighteth in the covenants of the Lord."
2 Nephi 11:5
Nephi felt joy in covenants long before temples as we know them existed. Temple covenants today continue that same ancient pattern: the Lord reaching out, inviting us into a relationship with Him. As I study, I feel how every book of scripture points toward that same covenant path.

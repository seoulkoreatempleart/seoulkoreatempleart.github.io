About the Artist

Hi! My name is Irene Yoon. In 2017, I served a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints at the Seoul Korea temple...

After my mission, I returned to Canada... I also took Korean folk art painting classes and tried painting the flowers from the temple gardens.

For the first few years, my art focused mainly on the Seoul Korea temple and its flowers.

My previous art and some temple photos may be seen at: https://seethetemple.wordpress.com/

Most of my photos of the Seoul Korea temple are at: https://www.instagram.com/seoulkoreatemple/

Videos of the Seoul temple that I took: www.youtube.com/@seethetemple

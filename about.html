Hi! My name is Irene Yoon. In 2017, I served a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints at the Seoul Korea Temple.

After my mission, I returned to Canada and continued painting. I also studied Korean folk art and enjoyed painting temple garden flowers.

For the first few years, my art focused mainly on the Seoul Korea Temple and its beautiful flowers.

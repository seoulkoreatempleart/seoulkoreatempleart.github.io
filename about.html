Hi! My name is Irene Yoon. In 2017, I served a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints at the Seoul Korea Temple. During my mission, I started drawing and painting the temple.

After my mission, I returned to Canada and continued drawing and painting. I took some private drawing and painting classes, including Korean folk art.

For the first few years, my art focused mainly on the Seoul Korea Temple and its beautiful flowers.
